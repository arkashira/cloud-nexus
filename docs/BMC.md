# Business Model Canvas – **cloud‑nexus**

| **Key Partners** | **Key Activities** | **Key Resources** |
|------------------|--------------------|-------------------|
| • Major public cloud providers (AWS, Azure, GCP) – joint‑go‑to‑market, API access, discount programs | • Continuous integration of cloud‑provider APIs and service catalogs | • Proprietary planning engine (graph‑based mapping of business goals → technical services) |
| • Enterprise ERP/CRM vendors (SAP, Salesforce) – data connectors | • AI‑assisted gap analysis & migration recommendation generation | • Large curated datasets (auto, instr‑resp, messages, system‑user‑assistant) for LLM fine‑tuning |
| • System integrators & MSPs – implementation partners | • Real‑time cost & risk simulation, “what‑if” scenario modeling | • Scalable cloud‑native micro‑service architecture (K8s, Terraform, Pulumi) |
| • Open‑source community (e.g., Terraform, Pulumi, Cloud Custodian) – contribution & plugin ecosystem | • Automated execution of approved migration/playbooks | • Multi‑tenant SaaS platform with RBAC, audit logging, compliance templates |
| • Compliance & security auditors (e.g., SOC2, ISO27001 bodies) | • Continuous compliance validation & reporting | • Sales & support organization (BD, PM, QA, Reviewer) |
| • Data providers (pricing APIs, carbon‑intensity feeds) | • Customer success onboarding & training workshops | • Intellectual property: patents on goal‑to‑service mapping algorithm |

| **Value Propositions** | **Customer Relationships** | **Channels** |
|------------------------|----------------------------|--------------|
| • **Strategic Alignment** – Translate high‑level business objectives (cost reduction, time‑to‑market, sustainability) into concrete cloud service blueprints. | • Dedicated Customer Success Managers for enterprise accounts. | • Direct sales force targeting CIO/CTO offices. |
| • **AI‑Powered Planning** – LLM‑driven recommendation engine that suggests optimal services, architectures, and migration paths. | • Self‑service portal with guided wizards and interactive dashboards. | • Partner ecosystem (MSPs, system integrators) delivering implementation services. |
| • **End‑to‑End Execution** – From plan approval to automated Terraform/Pulumi rollout, with rollback & drift detection. | • Quarterly business reviews & ROI reporting. | • Marketplace listings on AWS/Azure/GCP partner portals. |
| • **Cost & Risk Transparency** – Real‑time TCO, carbon‑footprint, and security risk scoring. | • Community forum & knowledge base powered by the company’s BRAIN (pgvector). | • Webinars, white‑papers, and industry conferences. |
| • **Compliance‑Ready** – Built‑in policy-as‑code templates for SOC2, ISO27001, GDPR, etc. | • SLA‑backed support (24/7 for premium tiers). | • API‑first integration for internal tooling (e.g., ITSM, ERP). |

| **Customer Segments** | **Revenue Streams** |
|-----------------------|---------------------|
| • Large enterprises undergoing digital transformation (≥ $500 M revenue). | • **Subscription SaaS** – Tiered plans (Starter, Professional, Enterprise) billed annually or monthly. |
| • Mid‑market firms adopting multi‑cloud strategies (>$100 M revenue). | • **Usage‑Based Fees** – Pay‑per‑run for automated execution (Terraform apply) and simulation runs. |
| • Managed Service Providers (MSPs) that resell the platform to their clients. | • **Professional Services** – Architecture workshops, custom connector development, migration assistance. |
| • Cloud‑focused consultancies & system integrators. | • **Marketplace Add‑ons** – Premium compliance packs, industry‑specific blueprints, carbon‑offset integrations. |
| • Government & regulated industries (finance, healthcare) needing audit‑ready migration. | • **Data & Insight Licenses** – Access to anonymized industry migration benchmarks derived from the BRAIN datasets. |

| **Cost Structure** |
|--------------------|
| • **R&D** – Salaries for AI/ML engineers, cloud architects, and platform developers; ongoing model fine‑tuning using the internal datasets (auto, instr‑resp, messages, system‑user‑assistant). |
| • **Cloud Infrastructure** – Compute, storage, and networking for multi‑tenant SaaS (K8s clusters, DB, vector store). |
| • **Partner & Licensing Fees** – Revenue share with cloud providers, compliance auditors, and data providers. |
| • **Sales & Marketing** – Enterprise sales team, partner enablement, events, digital campaigns. |
| • **Compliance & Security** – Audits, certifications (SOC2, ISO27001), security tooling. |
| • **Support & Customer Success** – Tiered support staff, training material production. |
| • **Operational Overheads** – Legal, HR, finance, and office costs. |
